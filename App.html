UNDERGRADUATE B.TECH

The Programs of study emphasize strong conceptual understanding and practical skills in their respective areas of specialization. All students are provided with a sound foundation in basic sciences, coupled with courses in the Humanities and Social Sciences in addition to courses of parent discipline.

POST GRADUATE M.TECH

The objective of the M. Tech Programs is to prepare professionals with advanced knowledge of the respective field who can serve industry, R&D organizations and can take up an academic career, including further studies in a relevant Ph. D Program.

INTEGRATED B.TECH M.TECH

Besides completing all requirements of the B.Tech program in the chosen branch, students have to take most of the courses of the respective Masters Program in the last one year. The Dual Degree Programs entitle the students to get both the Bachelors as well as the Masters Degree.

MBA COLLEGE IN NOIDA

During the 2 years program, students at JBS - JIIT undergo a rigorous 8 weeks corporate internship and 4 weeks of social internship to get better equipped with practical knowledge and get sensitized about social issues.
